## 1. The Convergence of Interests

Based on your historical project logs (AI Agents, News Tech, Unity, Web3, RL/Simulators) spanning from 2020 onwards, your interests are stacked layers of a single overarching vision. We can group them into three macro-pillars:

### Pillar A: The Builder Infrastructure (AI Coding)

- **What it is:** The tools that build the tools. AI-assisted coding frameworks (AgentOS, SpecKit, BMad).
    
- **Your Goal:** Create a hyper-efficient, self-reinforcing system to code your other highly complex technical projects.
    

### Pillar B: The Knowledge Graph (Second Brain & Ingestion)

- **What it is:** The **Ingestion Engine** (managing digital chaos, sifting, prioritizing via **DUPARA** - _Projects, Areas, Roles, Archives_) combined with a Context Engine (Zep, Graphiti).
    
- **The Applications:**
    
    - **Internal:** The Second Brain / Startup OS (A massive context engine resolving your historical research).
        
    - **External:** The **Geo-Temporal Event Simulator (News Hues Tech)**. Moving far beyond a standard news reader, this leverages data sources like GDELT, TABARI, and CAMEO to map global events onto an interactive spatial timeline, tracking real-world people as simulated "actors."
        

### Pillar C: The Spatial/Simulation Layer (AugmentoriaQuest)

- **What it is:** The physical/visual manifestation. Game AI Engines, Unity 3D behavior trees, geometric ML, and spatial scraping.
    
- **The Backbone:** **Smart Blockchain Objects**. Built on years of Reinforcement Learning (CS285) and Unity ML-Agents research. These are ML-trained behavioral capsules (encapsulating physical/animation logic) attached to 3D objects living on-chain. This relies heavily on synthetic data, simulators (Carla), and procedural generation to serve as the ultimate backbone for "Augmentoria Worlds."
